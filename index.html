HoursLogger

D&M DevCo, LLC Ruby on Rails Product

HoursLogger is Ruby on Rails product of D&M DevCo that is synced with QuickBooks Online to help consultants track their time and invoice clients. The app uses Ruby, Javascript, Haml, SCSS, MySQL, QuickBooks API, and jQuery. I helped sync their invoicing feature with the QuickBooks API so that users are able to create, edit, or print to PDF invoices within the app.
